# Code Of Conduct

This project follows the Contributor Covenant.

## Our pledge

We want the project to be welcoming, constructive, and safe for contributors of all experience levels.

## Expected behavior

- be respectful and curious
- assume good intent
- focus critique on code and ideas, not people
- help newer contributors succeed

## Unacceptable behavior

- harassment or discrimination
- personal attacks
- publishing private information
- repeated bad-faith disruption

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation when necessary to keep the community healthy.
